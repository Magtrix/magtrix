# Madhuri Agarwal

**Education Economist · What Works Hub for Global Education, University of Oxford**

I work at the intersection of rigorous evidence and education policy — with a focus on foundational learning, AI in education, and gender equity in low- and middle-income countries.

---

## What I Work On

**🔬 Research & Evidence Synthesis**
Generating and synthesising evidence on what improves foundational literacy and numeracy at scale in LMICs. My current work at the [What Works Hub for Global Education](https://www.wwhge.org/people/madhuri-agarwal/), Blavatnik School of Government, Oxford, focuses on the societal benefits of foundational learning and the evidence demand landscape across donors, governments, and implementation partners.

**🤖 AI in Foundational Learning**
Developing a policy framework for how governments in LMICs should approach AI in foundational learning programmes — mapped against the structured pedagogy model (materials + teacher PD + instructional coaching) that drives learning gains at scale. Member of [AIEOU](https://aieou.web.ox.ac.uk/node/4111406) (AI in Education: Outcomes and Understanding) at Oxford.

**⚖️ Gender, Education & Development**
Research on how education shapes women's lives — from reducing domestic violence to intergenerational investment in daughters. 

---

## Current Projects

- 📄 **AI in Foundational Learning: A Policy Framework for LMICs** — mapping AI use cases onto structured pedagogy components with enabling conditions and evidence standards for responsible scale-up
- 📊 **Global AI in Education Frameworks Analysis** — synthesising 16 frameworks (UNESCO, UNICEF, World Bank, OECD, Brookings and others) for what they offer LMIC foundational learning policymakers
- 📚 **Evidence synthesis on foundational learning** — what works, for whom, at what cost, and at what scale
